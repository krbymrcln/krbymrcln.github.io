Data Deletion Instructions

How to request removal of your data from Daloy's systems.

Effective date: January 1, 2026

How to request deletion

Send an email to info@daloy-ph.com with the subject line "Data Deletion". Include the phone number, name, or handle you used to message the business, so we can locate your record.

What gets removed

  • Your conversation history held in the automation's logs.
  • Your contact details and any lead record stored in the client business's Google Sheet or CRM, to the extent that business confirms removal on their end.
  • Any lead-scoring or qualification data derived from your conversation.

Records required for legal, accounting, or fraud-prevention purposes may be retained for the minimum period required by law, after which they are also deleted.

Timeframe

We process deletion requests within 30 days of receipt.

Confirmation

Once your data has been located and removed, we will reply to your email confirming that deletion is complete. If we're unable to locate a record matching the details you provided, we'll let you know so you can send additional identifying information.
