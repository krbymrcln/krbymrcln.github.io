The Problem

Most Philippine small businesses
fly blind.

Manual kuaderno tracking leads to spoiled goods, missed sales, and no proof of income when it's time to get a loan.

📋

Manual, error-prone records

Handwritten kuadernos get lost, soaked, or are never updated. Owners don't know what's really in stock until it's gone.

🗑️

Inventory waste eats margins

Overstocked perishables expire. Understocked fast movers cost sales. Without data, reordering is pure guesswork.

🏦

No paper trail, no loan

Banks ask for months of financial records. Most SMEs can't produce them — so they can't access the capital they need to grow.

The Solution

Everything a negosyante needs,
in one app.

⚡ Quick Entry

Record sales and restocks in seconds

A floating button opens a batch entry drawer. Type a product name, add +qty to restock or -qty to sell. Commit a whole day's movements in under a minute.

  • Autocomplete product search
  • Running stock balance validation — blocks oversells
  • Batch commit: record 20 movements at once
  • Create new products without leaving the drawer

🧠 AI Insights

Know before you run out — or overstock

IMBENTARYO analyzes your last 30 days of movements to predict stockouts and flag items at risk of spoiling or becoming dead stock.

  • Stockout countdown per product
  • Waste risk flagging (High / Medium / Low)
  • Recommended reorder quantity (2-week supply)
  • Estimated waste value in pesos
