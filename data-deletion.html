Data Deletion Instructions for Daloy

Last updated: July 10, 2026

If you have sent a message to a business Page that uses Daloy's automated assistant, and you would like your information deleted, you can request deletion in one of these ways:

  1. Email us at kerby.marcelino@gmail.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request." Include the Facebook Page you contacted and, if possible, the name you messaged from, so we can locate your information.
  2. We will process your deletion request within 30 days and confirm by email once your information has been removed.

If you have any questions about how your data is handled, please see our Privacy Policy or contact us at kerby.marcelino@gmail.com.
