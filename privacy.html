Privacy Policy

How Daloy Computer Programming Services collects, uses, and protects your information.

Effective date: January 1, 2026

Who we are

Daloy Computer Programming Services ("Daloy", "we", "us") is based in Metro Manila, Philippines. We build AI automation systems for small and medium businesses, including automated reply, lead-qualification, and routing tools that operate over Meta Messenger, Instagram, and Viber. You can reach us at info@daloy-ph.com.

What we collect

When a customer messages one of our clients' business accounts, our systems may process:

  • Name and profile identifiers made available by the messaging platform (Messenger, Instagram, Viber).
  • The content of messages sent to the business, so the AI can generate a relevant reply.
  • Contact details voluntarily shared in conversation (e.g. phone number, email, address, service preferences).
  • Basic interaction metadata (timestamps, message status) used for lead scoring and reporting.

We do not collect payment card numbers, government IDs, or other sensitive categories of data through these automations.

Why we collect it

Data is processed solely to:

  • Generate and send timely, relevant replies to inbound messages.
  • Score and qualify leads for the business we're automating on behalf of.
  • Log the conversation and lead details so the business can follow up.

Where it's stored

Lead and conversation data is written to the client business's own Google Sheets or CRM account — not a Daloy-owned database. The business you messaged controls and retains that record. Daloy retains only what is required to operate and troubleshoot the automation itself.

Who processes it

To deliver this service, message and lead data may pass through the following processors:

  • Meta / Facebook — the messaging platform (Messenger/Instagram) itself.
  • Anthropic — provides the AI model used to generate automated replies.
  • Google — Sheets/Workspace, where the client business stores lead records.

Each processor handles data under its own privacy terms in addition to this policy.

Retention

Conversation and lead data is retained for as long as the client business needs it for follow-up and reporting, or until a deletion request is received. Daloy's own operational logs are retained for a limited period for debugging and are not used for any purpose beyond operating the automation.

Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your data at any time. Because records are stored in the client business's own systems, we will coordinate with them to fulfil your request.

How to request deletion

Email info@daloy-ph.com with the subject line "Data Deletion," or see our Data Deletion Instructions page for full details.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as our services evolve. Material changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
