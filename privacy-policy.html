Privacy Policy for Daloy

Last updated: July 10, 2026

Daloy ("we", "us", "our") provides automated customer-service messaging tools for businesses on Facebook Messenger. This Privacy Policy explains what information we handle, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

Who we are

Daloy is operated by Kerby Marcelino, based in the Philippines. For any privacy questions, contact us at kerby.marcelino@gmail.com.

What information we collect

When a business installs our automated assistant on their Facebook Page, and a customer sends a message to that Page, we may process:

  • The content of messages sent to the business Page
  • The sender's Facebook-provided name and page-scoped ID (PSID)
  • Contact details a customer voluntarily provides (such as phone number, location, or preferred schedule)

We only process this information to provide the automated reply and lead-capture service to the business that owns the Page.

How we use information

  • To automatically reply to customer inquiries on behalf of the business
  • To record customer inquiry details so the business can follow up

We do not sell this information. We do not use it for advertising. We do not share it with third parties except as needed to operate the service (for example, the messaging platform and the business owner who receives the leads).

How we store information

Customer inquiry data is stored securely and is accessible only to the business that owns the Page and to Daloy for the purpose of operating the service. We retain data only as long as needed to provide the service, or as the business requires for follow-up.

Your rights

Customers may request access to, correction of, or deletion of their information. See our Data Deletion instructions, or contact us at kerby.marcelino@gmail.com.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be posted at this URL.
